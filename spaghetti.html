Spaghetti recipe

Descripton

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish that features cooked spaghetti noodles topped with a sauce made from tomatoes, garlic and herbs such as basil and oregano.

The dish can also be prepared with meatballs or other protein, and can be customized with additional ingredients such as cheese, vegetables, or mushrooms. Spaghetti is a beloved comfort food enjoyed around the world.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Chopped onion
  • Minced garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Beef broth or red win
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Grated parmesan chees(optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook the ground beef until browned and fully cooked. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet and cook until softened, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stir in the can of diced tomatoes (undrained), tomato paste, and dried basil. Simmer for 5-10 minutes until the sauce has thickened and the flavors have melded together.
  5. Season the sauce with salt and pepper to taste
  6. Serve the spaghetti noodles topped with the bolognese sauce. Optionally,sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese before serving
